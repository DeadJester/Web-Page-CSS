DIRECTIONS TO CUSTOMER SERVICE OFFICE:
Traveling from Phoenix area (including Sky Harbor Airport) take I-10 east and exit 167A (Queen Creek Rd.) and turn right. Continue south on Maricopa Rd. (Hwy 347) to the Maricopa city limits.
The customer service office is located on the right behind Great Western Bank. Turn right at the Shell gas station, and then take the first right into the parking lot.

DIRECTIONS TO ADMINISTRATION OFFICE:
From Phoenix (including Sky Harbor Airport) take I-10 east and exit 167A (Queen Creek Rd.) and turn right. Continue south on Maricopa Rd. (Hwy 347) though the City of Maricopa.
At 8.3 miles past the railroad crossing in Maricopa, (just south of Papago Road at approx. mile marker 166) turn left (east) on Louis Johnson Dr., approx. 2 miles from Hwy. 347 on the north side of Louis Johnson Dr.
