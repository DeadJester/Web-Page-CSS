ED3 Board of Directors meetings are normally held on a recurring basis on the 4th Wednesday of the month.  Agendas are posted to the website at least 24 hours prior to the meetings.

Please click the link below to view the Notice/Agenda for all upcoming Board Meetings.

The ED3 Board of Directors may elect to hold an Executive Session for any agenda item at any time during a meeting to discuss or consult with its legal counsel for legal advice on matters listed on an agenda pursuant to ARS § 38-431.03 A 3. 

Items on an agenda may be discussed out of the order shown, unless they have been specifically noted to be set for a certain time.
