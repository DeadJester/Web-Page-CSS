Level or Budget Payment Plan

Electrical District No. 3 is now offering two Programs to help manage your electric bill. The Level Payment Plan (LPP) and Budget Payment Plan (BPP).

Budget Payment Plan (BPP) - is a no fee Program that balances the seasonal highs and lows of your electric bill to make your payment more predictable each month. It can make household budgeting easier. If your usage remains similar to the past, the amount of your ED3 bill will remain constant each month all year long.

Level Payment Plan (LPP) - is a no fee Program that averages the most recent twelve (12) months of account history. Each month your power bill will be a different amount and will vary from month to month.
